About The Event

The Waste Wise Tunisia Solution Incubator is a collaborative event aimed at solving critical waste management challenges. Participants will work together to design innovative and scalable solutions for sustainable waste management. Organized in partnership with the Municipality of El Mourouj, this event brings together creative minds to inspire impactful change in Tunisia's waste ecosystem.

Event Venue

Event venue location info and gallery

South Mediterranean University - SMU, Tunisia

The Mediterranean School of Business (MSB), part of South Mediterranean University (SMU), was founded in 2004 and initially offered a part-time Executive MBA (EMBA) program. The first cohort included 43 executives. Today, MSB has over 900 EMBA alumni from 37 nationalities and offers undergraduate and Master's programs, with over 1000 students enrolled. These programs are accredited by EFMD and AMBA. The school is highly regarded, ranked among the top business schools in Africa.

Frequently Asked Question

  • What are the dates and venue for the Waste Wise Tunisia Hackathon?

    The hackathon will take place from 01 - 02 February 2025 at South Mediterranean University - SMU.

  • What is the theme of the Waste Wise Tunisia Hackathon?

    The hackathon focuses on three transformative challenges:

    • Mapping Establishments Open to the Public (EOP) for accurate, dynamic maps.
    • Optimizing Waste Collection Routes to streamline logistics.
    • Encouraging Household Waste Sorting to promote sustainable waste practices.

  • What kind of activities can participants expect during the event?

    The hackathon offers an immersive experience, including:

    • Workshops on data visualization, process automation, and more.
    • Mentorship sessions with industry experts.
    • Engaging activities like VR experiences, an artistic session, and a fun zone.

  • Who can participate in the hackathon?

    The hackathon is open to anyone passionate about innovation and sustainability, including students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts. Teams can consist of individuals from diverse backgrounds.

  • Are meals and accommodations provided?

    Yes, meals will be provided throughout the event. However, participants will be hosted in the hackathon venue during the event but feel free to bring all your confy gears with you.

  • What will teams gain from participating in this hackathon?

    Participants will gain:

    • Hands-on experience in solving real-world problems.
    • Opportunities to work with mentors and industry experts.
    • Exposure to cutting-edge tools and techniques.
    • A chance to win prizes and make a meaningful impact on sustainability.
    • Securing a potential contract to continue implementing your projects.
